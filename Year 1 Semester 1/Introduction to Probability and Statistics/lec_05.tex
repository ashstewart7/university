\section{Coin Flips}
Flipping a coin is one of the simplist distributions we can create.

Given a:
\begin{align*}
P(H) &= 0.5\\
P(T) &= 0.5
\end{align*}

We know that $P(HHHH) = 0.5^4$.

And $P(\text{Three Heads and One Tail}) = P(\text{HHHT or HHTH or THHH or HTHH}) = 4 \times 0.5^4$
